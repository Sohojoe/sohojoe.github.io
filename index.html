About Us

Vidya Gamer was founded by Joe Booth, an Executive Producer and Creative Director at the intersection of traditional video games and Games and Learning.

Joe brings 25+ years experience on major industry franchises (FIFA, Need For Speed, Ghost Recon, Roller Coaster Tycoon), with over 24 million games sold generating $1b of retail revenue and 10 AIAS & BAFTA nominations.

Our first app, Crowded Fiction, was the #3 rated app of 2013 (qualityIndex.com).
